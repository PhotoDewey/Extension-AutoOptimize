# Auto Optimize

**One-click light and colour correction for your photos.**

---

## What it does

Auto Optimize analyses each photo and fixes the three most common problems in a single click:

- **Flat or washed-out colours** — levels are stretched so every channel uses the full tonal range
- **Too dark or too bright overall** — mid-tone brightness is shifted toward a natural 50% average
- **Colour casts** — unwanted tints are removed using automatic white balance

Each correction can also be applied individually from the adjustments panel, so you stay in control.

---

## Features

- **Auto Levels** — per-channel histogram stretch that clips the darkest 1 % and brightest 1 % of pixels for a clean result without blown highlights
- **Gamma Correct** — automatic mid-tone brightness correction that targets a natural average luminance
- **Auto Colors** — gray-world white balance that neutralises colour casts without touching overall brightness

---

## How to use

1. Open a photo in PhotoDewey
2. Open the **Auto Optimize** panel in the sidebar
3. Click **Auto Optimize** to apply all three corrections at once, or toggle each correction on individually
4. The edit is added to your history — undo or remove it at any time
